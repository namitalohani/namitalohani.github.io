NDTV India · Budget 2024 Campaign
50+ graphics in a single day — Budget 2024 content production
On Union Budget day 2024, I was the primary graphic designer for NDTV India's social media coverage. I independently conceptualised, researched, and designed over 50 graphics — published live across NDTV India's handles as the Budget was being presented. I also created the master Canva templates that the wider team used throughout the day, enabling the entire team's visual output. Every data point personally verified, every design built from scratch under live deadline pressure.

What this actually means: Budget day is one of the most intense news events of the year — dozens of announcements in rapid succession, each needing to be understood, verified, designed, and published before the news cycle moves on. Producing 50+ graphics under those conditions, solo, is not a design task. It's editorial judgement, data literacy, and production speed working simultaneously.

IIMC · Hindi Journalism Department
Jan Jagriti — a 4-page Hindi newspaper, produced end to end

As part of IIMC's practical journalism training, our cohort produced Jan Jagriti — a full Hindi newspaper across 7 editions. Every issue was written, edited, fact-checked, and laid out entirely by students. I taught myself Adobe InDesign during this process and became the primary layout designer for the team — while also training classmates to use it. Across editions I rotated through every role: reporter, sub-editor, layout designer, and editor.

About me

The longer version — for people who actually read.

I'm a journalist, digital strategist, and self-taught creative based in Lucknow. My career has taken me from IIMC's newsroom training to NDTV's live election coverage — and the through-line has always been the same: find the story, then find the best way to tell it.

I have two master's degrees — one in Journalism & Mass Communication, one in Modern History — and a habit of teaching myself whatever tool a job requires. Adobe Premiere Pro, Tableau, AI integrations — no classroom, just curiosity and deadlines.

I speak Hindi, English, Sanskrit, and enough German to get myself in trouble. I write on Medium, play chess, and believe the best stories are always one layer deeper than the obvious one.
